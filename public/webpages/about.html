About Textbook Fetcher

Lindsey Kvarfordt, James Trotter, Kerry Vance, Kawin Pechetratanapanit

Textbook Fetcher was developed as a final project for Oregon State University's Fall 2018 CS290 course, led by Rob Hess.

Data is fetched from Carnegie Mellon University and Massachusettes Institute of Technology.

Lindsey wrote the client-side JS. James wrote the front end HTML and CSS. Vance and Kawin worked on the back end, fetching the data from the sources and compiling them into a unified JSON object.
